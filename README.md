## About Labtrum

Labtrum is a lab management app for life-science research teams. Version 1.0 focuses on animal colony management, including animals, cages, racks, breeding records, and IACUC protocols. Labtrum is built natively on iPhone with iCloud sync; no third-party servers, no analytics, no advertising.
